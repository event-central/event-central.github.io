Our mission

Help students build community by creating a digital platform where they can find events to meet other likeminded students.

Why EventCentral?

Unique value propositon: We offer more features than our main competitor, including the ability to purchase tickets in-app, create highly-promoted event posts, and access to in-depth analytics.

Unfair advantage: Our application is created by UMass students, for UMass students. We are building are application with feedback from real UMass students via surveys.

Commitment to PIT: EventCentral promotes data protection and user security, allowing users to know that their data is safe and protected while using our application.

Our prices

Premium tier - $5/Month

Organizer tier - $15/Month

  • In-depth attendance metrics.
